Frequently Asked Questions

This section resolves all your queries that you have in your mind related to the Academies and their working.

  • Are the Courses Paid / Free in the Academies?

    The Academies are run at the College of Computing FREE OF COST. The students can get the benefit of earning free of cost certificates and learn with the Global Software Leading Platforms for their Career.

  • Can i have multiple courses at the same time?

    You can opt for multiple courses and can study in parallel several streams as well. You have to manage all the pre-requisites to complete the courses and get certified.

  • Are the classes / session self paced or instructor lead?

    Some of the classes in the academies are lead by instructors and some of them are self paced. It is also worth to mention that several courses are blended in nature with self paced and instructor led functionality together.

  • How to get the certificates once I complete a course?

    Once the course is completed the Academies provide the certificates directly from the Portal and you will recieve it in email. For any issues related to the certificates you can connect back to the Academy Admin Office.

  • What if I am unable to complete the courses on time?

    For self paced courses the academies can help you to increase a part of the time needed for the course completion, however for instructor led courses you have match with the timeline given by the instructor.
